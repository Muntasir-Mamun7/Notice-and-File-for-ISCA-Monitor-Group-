### 📸 Sports Culture Events
*(Submission deadline: **May 31, 2026** — Email: **dingf@njupt.edu.cn**)*

1. **📷 Photography Competition**
   - Theme: Athletes, coaches, referees, and competition highlights
   - Spirit: "Love Sport • NJUPT Future" / "信达天下，自强不息"
   - Max **5 photos** per college; each photo must be **≥ 2MB**
   - No heavy editing/digital manipulation allowed

2. **📰 News Report Competition**
   - Publish via WeChat articles, official Weibo, or campus website
   - Any topic related to the event
   - Minimum **1 news article** per college

3. **🗣️ Sports Slogan Competition**
   - Create slogans featuring: "NJUPT", "Sports", "Exercise", "Spirit"
   - Max **3 slogans** per college

### 📋 Eligibility
- Must be a **currently enrolled undergraduate or graduate student** in good physical health
- Teams are organized **by college/school**
- All athletes **must carry their student ID or national ID** on competition day

### 📝 Registration Rules
- Each student may enter a **maximum of 2 events**
- Fun events: **maximum 1 event** per student
- Registration requirements for each sport will be announced by the respective organizing committee
